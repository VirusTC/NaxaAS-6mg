[1, 2]

The comprehensive table below expands on these clusters by indexing every validated clinical sign, symptom subset, diagnostic indicator, and sub-acute manifestation documented across federal [site:gov] and academic [site:edu] domains, directly mapping them to the specific molecular and enzymatic mechanisms of cupric ($\text{Cu}^{2+}$) repletion.
